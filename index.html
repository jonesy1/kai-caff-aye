about us

Kai Caff Aye are a locally owned eatery in Rotorua, with a menu that is based around their love of Kai (food). With the menu items available, Kai Caff Aye wanted the cafe to represent who they and their staff are (Maori Culture) and what they enjoy eating. Offering exceptional service and sharing the local Maori Culture is what Kai Caff Aye is very passionate about. Dine in or pickup to takeaway by using the Order Online button above.
